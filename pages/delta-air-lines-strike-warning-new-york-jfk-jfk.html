What Delta Air Lines Owes You Under DOT Rules 2026

If Delta Air Lines cancels your flight, they must provide a refund or rebook you on the next available flight. If the cancellation occurs within 24 hours of the original flight time, or if the flight is significantly delayed, the airline may also be required to provide meal vouchers or accommodation, depending on the length of the delay and the time of day. For example, if your flight from New York JFK to Los Angeles (LAX) is canceled, and the next available flight is the following day, Delta Air Lines may provide a hotel voucher and meal compensation, in addition to rebooking you on the next flight.

In the case of a delay, the airline must provide updates on the status of your flight and may be required to provide compensation if the delay exceeds a certain threshold, typically 3 hours for domestic flights. For international flights, such as from New York JFK to London (LHR), the delay threshold for compensation is usually 4 hours. It's essential to keep in mind that these rules apply to all Delta Air Lines flights, including those departing from New York JFK.
